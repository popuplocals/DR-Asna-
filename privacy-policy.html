This Privacy Policy explains how this website (asnanaqvi.com), operated on behalf of Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi, collects, uses and protects information when you visit or interact with it.

Information We Collect

This is a static informational website. It does not run its own patient database or store medical records. Information may be collected in the following ways:

  • Contact details you choose to share — if you call, WhatsApp, or email us directly using the contact details on this site, we receive whatever information you provide in that conversation.
  • Appointment booking — appointment booking is handled entirely by Apollo 24|7 (apollo247.com), an independent third-party platform. Any information you submit there is governed by Apollo's own privacy policy, not this one.
  • Basic usage data — like most websites, standard hosting/analytics infrastructure may log non-identifying technical data such as browser type, pages visited and approximate location, used only to understand site usage and improve performance.

How Information Is Used

Any contact information you share directly with the clinic (phone, WhatsApp, email) is used solely to respond to your enquiry or manage your appointment. We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

This website links to and embeds third-party services, including Google Maps, Google Fonts, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp and Apollo 24|7. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies when you interact with them. We encourage you to review those policies independently.

Cookies

This site does not use its own tracking cookies. Embedded third-party content (such as Google Maps or YouTube videos) may set cookies in accordance with their own policies.

Your Rights

You may request details of any information you have shared with the clinic directly, or request that it be deleted, by contacting us using the details on our Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in how the website operates. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi's clinic via the details on the Contact page.
