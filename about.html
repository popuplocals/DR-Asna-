Know Your Doctor

Compassionate, Internationally Trained Women's Health Care

Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi is a Senior Consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology with over 10 years of clinical experience, currently practising at Apollomedics Super Speciality Hospitals, Lucknow. As a Member of the Royal College of Obstetricians & Gynaecologists, London (MRCOG) with a Diploma in IVF & Reproductive Medicine, she brings international training to her practice. She is widely appreciated by her patients for being polite, knowledgeable and humble — and for giving every patient the time they need to explain their concerns.

Her approach combines international clinical standards with warm, patient-first care, making her a trusted name for women's health in Lucknow.

Philosophy of Care

Dr. Naqvi believes that great medicine begins with listening. She takes time to understand each patient's history and concerns, explains every option clearly, and builds a treatment plan that respects each woman's individual needs and comfort.
