Know Your Doctor

Compassionate, Internationally Trained Women's Health Care

Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi is a Senior Consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology with over 10 years of clinical experience, currently practising at Apollomedics Super Speciality Hospitals, Lucknow. She is widely appreciated by her patients for being polite, knowledgeable and humble — and for giving every patient the time they need to explain their concerns.

After completing her MBBS and MS in Obstetrics & Gynaecology at the Institute of Medical Sciences, Banaras Hindu University (IMS–BHU), Varanasi, she earned Membership of the Royal College of Obstetricians & Gynaecologists, London (MRCOG) and a Diploma in IVF & Reproductive Medicine. With more than a decade of clinical experience — including 6+ years within the UK's NHS — and earlier work at University Hospital Schleswig-Holstein, Germany, she brings a genuinely global standard of care to women in Lucknow.

Her clinical interests span the full journey of women's health — high-risk and normal pregnancy, painless delivery, infertility and IVF, laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery, PCOS and hormonal disorders, and menopausal and adolescent care. Whatever stage of life brings you to her clinic, her aim is the same: an accurate diagnosis, clear choices and a treatment plan that respects your comfort and your wishes.
