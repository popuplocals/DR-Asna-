Know Your Doctor

Compassionate, Internationally Trained Women's Health Care

Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi is a Senior Consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology with over 10 years of clinical experience, currently practising at Apollomedics Super Speciality Hospitals, Lucknow. As a Member of the Royal College of Obstetricians & Gynaecologists, London (MRCOG) with a Diploma in IVF & Reproductive Medicine, she brings international training to her practice. She is widely appreciated by her patients for being polite, knowledgeable and humble — and for giving every patient the time they need to explain their concerns.

Her clinical interests span the full journey of women's health — high-risk and normal pregnancy, painless delivery, infertility and IVF, laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery, PCOS and hormonal disorders, and menopausal and adolescent care. Whatever stage of life brings you to her clinic, her aim is the same: an accurate diagnosis, clear choices and a treatment plan that respects your comfort and your wishes.

Her approach combines international clinical standards with warm, patient-first care — making her a trusted name for women's health in Lucknow. With the advanced facilities of Apollomedics behind her, she ensures every patient receives safe, modern and compassionate treatment under one roof.
