A normal (vaginal) delivery is the most natural way to welcome your baby — and with modern painless-delivery techniques, it can also be a calm and comfortable experience. Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi supports mothers in Lucknow through safe, closely monitored normal deliveries with effective, well-planned pain relief, so you can focus on meeting your baby.

Benefits of Normal Delivery

  • Faster recovery and shorter hospital stay
  • Lower risk of surgical complications
  • Immediate skin-to-skin contact and easier breastfeeding
  • Long-term health benefits for the baby

What Is Painless (Epidural) Delivery?

Epidural analgesia greatly reduces labour pain while keeping you awake and active during birth. Dr. Asna and the anaesthesia team assess each mother individually to plan safe, effective relief tailored to you.

What to Expect

  • Birth-preparation counselling during pregnancy
  • Continuous monitoring of mother and baby in labour
  • Pain-relief options discussed and chosen in advance
  • Gentle support through each stage of labour
  • Postnatal care and breastfeeding guidance

Normal Delivery After a C-Section (VBAC)

Many women who have had a previous caesarean can safely attempt a vaginal birth (VBAC). Dr. Asna assesses your history individually and advises whether VBAC is a safe option for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is painless delivery safe for the baby?

Yes — epidural analgesia is widely used and considered safe, planned and monitored by the obstetric and anaesthesia team.

Does an epidural always work?

It is highly effective for most women; the team adjusts the approach to your needs.

Can I have a normal delivery after a previous C-section?

In many cases a VBAC is possible; it is assessed individually based on your history.
