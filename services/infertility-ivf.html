Infertility Treatment & IVF

Home / Services / Infertility / IVF

Difficulty conceiving can be emotionally challenging, but most couples can be helped with the right evaluation and treatment. With a Diploma in IVF & Reproductive Medicine from Germany, Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi provides sensitive, evidence-based fertility care for both partners.

Fertility evaluation

  • Detailed history and assessment for both partners
  • Hormonal and ovulation testing
  • Ultrasound and tubal patency assessment
  • Semen analysis and male-factor evaluation guidance

Treatment options

  • Ovulation induction and cycle monitoring
  • Lifestyle and PCOS management to improve fertility
  • Intrauterine insemination (IUI) guidance
  • In-vitro fertilisation (IVF) and assisted reproduction pathways

Every plan is tailored to the couple's diagnosis, age and preferences, with clear counselling at each step.
