When a C-Section May Be Advised

  • Baby in breech or transverse position
  • Placenta previa or other placental concerns
  • Labour not progressing safely
  • Fetal distress requiring urgent delivery
  • Certain maternal conditions or previous C-sections

Planned vs Emergency C-Section

A planned (elective) C-section is scheduled in advance when a vaginal birth is known to carry risk, allowing calm preparation. An emergency C-section is performed when concerns arise during labour. Dr. Asna prepares you for both possibilities so there are no surprises on the day.
