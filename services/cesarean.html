Cesarean Section (C-Section)

Home / Services / Cesarean (C-Section)

When a vaginal birth carries risk for the mother or baby, a cesarean section is a safe and well-established way to deliver. Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi performs both planned and emergency C-sections with meticulous surgical care and complete support before, during and after delivery.

When a C-section may be advised

  • Baby in breech or transverse position
  • Placenta previa or other placental concerns
  • Failure of labour to progress safely
  • Fetal distress requiring urgent delivery
  • Certain maternal medical conditions or previous C-sections

Our approach

  • Thorough counselling so you understand the reasons and process
  • Modern anaesthesia for a comfortable experience
  • Careful surgical technique to support good healing
  • Dedicated postnatal recovery and newborn care

Why choose Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi in Lucknow

Whether your C-section is planned or becomes necessary during labour, Dr. Naqvi keeps you informed and in control. With over a decade of surgical experience and full theatre and newborn-care support at Apollomedics, she focuses on a safe delivery and a smooth, comfortable recovery for you and your baby.
