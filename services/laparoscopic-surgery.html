Laparoscopic (Minimally Invasive) Surgery

Home / Services / Laparoscopic Surgery

Laparoscopic surgery uses small keyhole incisions and a camera-guided instrument instead of a large open cut. With a Fellowship in Laparoscopic Surgery, Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi offers advanced minimally invasive procedures that mean less pain, minimal scarring and a quicker return to normal life.

Conditions treated laparoscopically

  • Ovarian cysts and benign ovarian tumours
  • Uterine fibroids (myomectomy)
  • Endometriosis
  • Ectopic pregnancy
  • Hysterectomy (removal of the uterus) where indicated

Why choose minimally invasive surgery

  • Smaller incisions and minimal scarring
  • Less post-operative pain
  • Shorter hospital stay
  • Faster recovery and return to daily activities
  • Lower risk of wound infection
