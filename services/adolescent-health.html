Why Early Guidance Matters

Addressing period and hormonal concerns early — like PCOS in the teenage years — can prevent bigger problems later and helps girls build a healthy, confident relationship with their bodies.

Why Choose Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi

Dr. Naqvi understands that a first gynaecology visit can feel daunting for a teenager. She creates a comfortable, respectful environment so young patients feel safe to ask questions and get the right care early.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should a teenage girl first see a gynaecologist?

When there are concerns like irregular or very painful periods, or for puberty guidance.

Are visits confidential?

Yes — care is handled sensitively, with privacy respected and family involved as appropriate.

Can teenagers have PCOS?

Yes — early diagnosis and lifestyle support help manage it well.

Is some period irregularity normal at first?

Some irregularity is common in the first couple of years; persistent problems should be checked.
