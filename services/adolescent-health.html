Home / Services / Adolescent Health

The teenage years bring many physical and hormonal changes that can raise questions and concerns. Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi provides a safe, sensitive and confidential space for adolescent girls and their parents to address health issues early.

Concerns we address

  • Irregular, painful or heavy periods
  • Early or delayed puberty
  • PCOS in teenagers
  • Menstrual hygiene and general health education
  • Acne, weight and hormonal changes

Our approach

  • Gentle, age-appropriate consultations
  • Reassurance and clear education for the young patient and family
  • Early detection and management of hormonal issues
  • Guidance on nutrition, lifestyle and wellbeing
