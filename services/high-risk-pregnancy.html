A high-risk pregnancy requires closer monitoring and specialised expertise to keep both mother and baby safe. With international training (MRCOG, London) and over a decade of experience, Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi provides comprehensive, reassuring care for complex pregnancies.

When is a pregnancy considered high-risk?

  • Maternal age below 18 or above 35
  • Pre-existing conditions such as diabetes, hypertension or thyroid disorders
  • Multiple pregnancy (twins or more)
  • Previous pregnancy complications or recurrent miscarriage
  • Pregnancy-related conditions like pre-eclampsia or gestational diabetes

How Dr. Naqvi helps

  • Detailed pre-pregnancy and antenatal counselling
  • Regular growth scans and fetal monitoring
  • Management of maternal medical conditions during pregnancy
  • Coordinated, safe delivery planning with the hospital team
  • 24/7 emergency and maternity support
