Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is one of the most common hormonal disorders in women and a frequent cause of irregular periods. Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi helps women understand and manage PCOS to protect long-term health and fertility.

Common symptoms

  • Irregular, infrequent or prolonged periods
  • Weight gain or difficulty losing weight
  • Excess hair growth or acne
  • Difficulty conceiving

Diagnosis & management

  • Clinical assessment, hormonal blood tests and ultrasound
  • Personalised lifestyle and diet guidance
  • Medication to regulate cycles and manage symptoms
  • Fertility support when planning pregnancy
  • Long-term monitoring for diabetes and metabolic health

Why choose Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi in Lucknow

PCOS is common and very manageable with the right guidance. Dr. Naqvi looks beyond the symptoms to the underlying hormonal picture, combining practical lifestyle advice with proven medical treatment so you can restore regular cycles, ease symptoms and protect your long-term health.
