Ovarian Cyst Treatment

Home / Services / Ovarian Cyst Treatment

Ovarian cysts are fluid-filled sacs that develop on or within an ovary. Many are harmless and resolve on their own, but some need treatment. Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi accurately evaluates ovarian cysts and recommends the safest, least invasive option for each patient.

Symptoms to watch for

  • Pelvic pain or pressure, often on one side
  • Bloating or abdominal fullness
  • Irregular periods
  • Pain during intercourse

Diagnosis & treatment

  • Ultrasound and, when needed, blood tests for accurate assessment
  • Watchful waiting for small, simple cysts
  • Medication to manage symptoms or prevent new cysts
  • Laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery for larger or persistent cysts

Why choose Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi in Lucknow

Most ovarian cysts are harmless, but the right assessment matters. Dr. Naqvi uses careful ultrasound evaluation to tell apart cysts that simply need watching from those that need treatment — and where surgery is required, she favours minimally invasive (laparoscopic) techniques for a faster, gentler recovery.
