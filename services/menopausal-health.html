Home / Services / Menopausal Health

Menopause is a natural stage of life, but its symptoms can affect comfort, mood and long-term health. Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi offers caring, individualised support to help women navigate the menopausal transition and stay healthy afterwards.

Common menopausal symptoms

  • Hot flushes and night sweats
  • Irregular periods leading up to menopause
  • Mood changes, anxiety or sleep disturbance
  • Vaginal dryness and urinary symptoms

How we help

  • Symptom assessment and lifestyle guidance
  • Hormone replacement therapy (HRT) where appropriate
  • Bone health and osteoporosis prevention
  • Heart health and routine screening advice
