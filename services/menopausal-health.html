Common Menopause Symptoms

  • Hot flashes and night sweats
  • Irregular periods during perimenopause
  • Mood changes, anxiety or low sleep
  • Vaginal dryness or discomfort
  • Reduced bone density and joint aches
  • Heart-health changes

How Dr. Asna Supports You

  • Lifestyle, diet and bone-health guidance
  • Hormone (HRT) therapy where suitable and safe
  • Non-hormonal options for those who prefer them
  • Prompt assessment of any post-menopausal bleeding
  • Long-term preventive care and screening

Why Post-Menopausal Bleeding Should Always Be Checked

Any bleeding after menopause is not normal and should be assessed promptly. Most causes are benign, but early evaluation rules out anything serious and gives peace of mind.
