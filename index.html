Know Your Doctor

Internationally Trained, Patient-First Women's Health Specialist

Dr. Asna Zehra Naqvi is a compassionate Senior Consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology with over 10 years of experience. She holds an MS in Obstetrics & Gynaecology, the prestigious MRCOG membership from the Royal College of Obstetricians & Gynaecologists, London, and a Diploma in IVF & Reproductive Medicine.

Practising at Apollomedics Super Speciality Hospitals, Lucknow, she focuses on high-risk pregnancy management, fertility & IVF care, and minimally invasive (laparoscopic) gynaecological surgery. From a young woman's first visit to pregnancy, delivery and menopause, she cares for women through every stage of life.

Patients value her calm, unhurried manner — she listens carefully, explains every option in plain language and never rushes an appointment, so you always leave feeling heard, informed and reassured.
